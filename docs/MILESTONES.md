# Suggested Milestones

## Testnet Stability

Focus: operational stability, deposit/withdrawal correctness, indexer/API reliability, websocket recovery.

## Blob DA Permanent Activation

Focus: repeated controlled blob runs, beacon verification, lifecycle monitoring, DA mismatch alerts, mandatory Blob DA activation.

## Audit Preparation

Focus: repository cleanup, scope docs, storage layout docs, threat model, test coverage, CI/CD, external audit package.

## Fraud-Proof Foundation

Focus: dispute skeleton, invalid-root detector, bond accounting, challenge/finality wiring, L1-first reconstruction verifier.

## Full Fault-Proof VM Research

Focus: deterministic execution, state witness/preimage model, step trace, bisection protocol, on-chain resolution.

## Mainnet Readiness

Focus: audits, multisig/timelock, production monitoring, secrets management, incident response, release runbooks.
